My name is Jessica Parker Gilbert and I am a graphic designer / art director based in Chicago. I have years of experience in print and have expanded into web design and e-mail marketing.

Current projects range from an upstart fashion magazine’s Web site to a weekly newspaper. Previously, I worked at the St. Petersburg Times, Florida's largest daily newspaper. I won a silver award and many awards of excellence from SND for my portfolio and individual pages.
